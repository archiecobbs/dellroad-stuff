    public abstract class SimpleKeyedContainer<I,T>
extends AbstractSimpleContainer<I,T>
    An AbstractSimpleContainer where the item IDs are generated from the items themselves by the subclass-provided method getKeyFor(T).

    Restriction: instances can never contain two objects whose keys are equal (in the sense of Object.equals(java.lang.Object)).
